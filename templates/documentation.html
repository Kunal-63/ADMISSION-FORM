Admission Information

Admission Guidelines

Welcome to our school! Here are the guidelines for nursery admission as per CBSE:

  • Age Criteria: Children should be between 3 to 4 years as of September 1st.
  • Documents Required: Birth certificate, residence proof, parent's ID, etc.
  • Admission Procedure: Fill the online form, submit documents, attend interview.

Payment Guidelines

For your convenience, we use HDFC's secure payment gateway for fee payments. Follow these guidelines:

  1. Visit our official website and log in to your parent portal.
  2. Select the payment option and choose HDFC as the payment gateway.
  3. Enter the required details, including student ID and payment amount.
  4. Proceed to payment and securely complete the transaction.
